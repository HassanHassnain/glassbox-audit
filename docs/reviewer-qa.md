# Reviewer Q&A

## Did Glassbox find a circuit?

No. The component/path analysis supports residual-stream localization, but it does not establish a full path or head-level mechanism with necessity, sufficiency, specificity, and replication.

## Did SAE beat the simple baselines?

No under the preregistered H3 criterion. SAE features beat matched random-SAE controls, but mean-difference/probe baselines remain stronger under held-out direct comparisons.

## Is the Qwen2.5-3B result a full replication?

It is partial Qwen-family replication: a late-layer effect appears, but specificity fails because benign/capability shifts are too large.

## Are Gemma or Llama results completed?

No. Their configs are included only as prepared replication recipes.
